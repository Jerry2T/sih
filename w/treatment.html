Treatment Advice for Diseased Plants

Our AI-driven analysis suggests the following treatment plan for the detected disease in your plant:

Recommended Pesticide Usage

Based on the severity of the disease, the following pesticide treatment is recommended:

  • Disease Severity: Moderate
  • Recommended Pesticide: Neem Oil
  • Percentage of Pesticide Solution: 2%
  • Application Frequency: Apply every 7 days until symptoms improve.

Additional Treatment Steps

In addition to pesticide application, consider the following steps to ensure plant recovery:

  • Ensure the plant is receiving adequate sunlight and water.
  • Remove and destroy any affected leaves or branches to prevent further spread.
  • Maintain good air circulation around the plant to reduce moisture buildup.
  • Monitor the plant closely for any changes in condition.

If symptoms persist or worsen, please consult a professional for further advice.
